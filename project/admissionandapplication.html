Application Procedures

Follow these steps to complete your admission process:

  1. Prepare Required Documents
    • Copy of IC / Passport
    • Academic transcripts (SPM / STPM / Diploma / Foundation)
    • Passport-sized photo
    • English test results (if applicable)
  2. Fill in the Online Application Form
    • Visit the Admission & Application page
    • Complete all required fields
    • Upload supporting documents
  3. Submit the Application
    • Review your details before submission
    • You will receive a confirmation email
  4. Attend an Interview (if required)
    • Some programmes may require an interview or placement test
    • Applicants will be notified via email
  5. Receive the Offer Letter
    • Successful applicants will receive an official offer letter
    • Follow the instructions to accept the offer
  6. Register & Make Payment
    • Complete the registration process
    • Pay the necessary fees
    • Prepare for orientation week
